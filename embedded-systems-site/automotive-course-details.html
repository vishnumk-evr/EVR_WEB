What You'll Learn

Automotive Protocols

  • FlexRay and MOST protocols
  • UDS (Unified Diagnostic Services)
  • AUTOSAR framework basics
ECU Development
  • Engine control unit (ECU) programming

  • Power management strategies
  • Real-time monitoring systems
